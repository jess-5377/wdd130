Our Story

Founded with a passion for adventure and love for the river, Splash White Water Rafting has been guiding thrill-seekers for over 20 years. We pride ourselves on safety, fun, and creating moments you'll treasure forever.

From serene scenic floats to heart-pounding rapids, our trips cater to all levels of experience. Every journey with us is an opportunity to bond with nature and with each other.
