Manila, Philippines

Manila, the capital of the Philippines, is a bustling 500-year old metropolis located on the eastern shore of Manila Bay. Known for its rich history, vibrant culture, and colonial architecture, the city blends the old and the new—featuring historic sites like Intramuros and Rizal Park alongside modern shopping centers, skyscrapers, and culinary hotspots such as Binondo and Makati Business District. As a major economic, political, and cultural hub, Manila plays a central role in the country’s development and remains a gateway to exploring the diverse islands of the Philippines.
